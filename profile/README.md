## Who needs us

We are not chasing volume. **We reserve effort for mandates where seriousness of outcome matches seriousness of inquiry**—and where a precise engineering response can materially change throughput, credibility, resilience or margin.

If that describes your situation, **you probably need someone who researches you before prescribing software** rather than quoting a SKU from a brochure.

### First we research—and only then propose work

Discovery is deliberately slow where it protects capital later.

1. **Context & stakes** — what the organisation sells, whom it serves, where margin concentrates, regulatory or contractual sensitivities you cannot afford to treat casually and what constitutes “broken” versus merely inconvenient today.
2. **People & choreography** — who owns decisions, where information currently lives, handoffs across sales, fulfilment and finance and which roles carry implicit knowledge that must not remain trapped in DMs or memory.
3. **Systems & artefacts** — the web estate(s), spreadsheets, portals, bookkeeping, ticketing, calendars, ERP or inventory shadows, integrations that “sort of work”, and telemetry you already possess or lack altogether.
4. **Customer-facing physics** — the actual journey someone takes from suspicion to retained revenue: search, referrals, outbound, forms, onboarding, invoicing—and where abandonment or mistrust aligns with observable failure (speed, correctness, reassurance).
5. **Technical truth** — where invited, crawling, benchmarks, latency checks, crawl debt, Core Web Vitals posture and rudimentary architectural review illuminate what prose alone cannot—as long as measurements stay tied back to consequence, not curiosity for its own sake.
6. **Competitive posture** — not vanity benchmarking, but the minimum standard your buyers already receive elsewhere and the delta you must surpass to deserve attention beyond price.

Artifacts from this phase may combine narrative synthesis, phased recommendations, sequencing of riskiest assumptions and—as appropriate—prioritised remediation before any net-new ambition. **If we cannot articulate the gap honestly, we will say so.**

### What “the gap” tends to resemble

Rarely does the gap advertise itself coherently.

| Pattern | Often sounds like internally | Where research usually surfaces friction |
|:-----|:----------------------------|:----------------------------------------|
| **Fragmentation** | “We duct-tape spreadsheets to half a CRM” | Ownership blur; duplicate entry; brittle syncs nobody trusts |
| **Opacity** | “Nobody knows conversion after the inquiry” | No single layer where pipeline, SLA and attribution meet |
| **Credibility lag** | “We look smaller than we are—or bigger than our systems tolerate” | Mismatch among brand, onboarding and reality of delivery |
| **Mobility choke** | “Field teams reinvent the playbook daily” | Intake optimised for desktops in an office—not techs truckside |
| **Platform ceiling** | “We bent the SaaS twice; we’re patching the third rupture now” | Off-the-rack choreography fighting your actual rules of business |
| **Operational drag** | “Engineers could build it; they’re busy keeping lights on” | Ambition without guarded capacity—isolated heroics instead of design |

Closing the breach is seldom *only* cosmetic: it blends **experience design**, sound **architecture**, humane **integrations** and pragmatic **migration** staged so interruption does not overwhelm the teams who must inhabit the outcome.

### Representative contexts—not an exhaustive taxonomy

Think of these as sketches of mandates where disciplined research tended to illuminate the widest delta between aspiration and tooling.

<details>
<summary><strong>Skilled trades & field-service industries</strong></summary>

**Symptoms voiced:** crews and vehicles run; dispatch rarely matches true capacity; callers repeat themselves; estimating lives in too many places; invoices drift behind completed work.

**What research sharpens:** how demand actually arrives (peaks, emergencies, planned work, project phases); where trust is conferred (reviews, licences, certifications, realistic ETAs); choke between what you promise crews and what systems can prove.

**Closing the breach:** disciplined intake calibrated to urgency; truthful scheduling realism; artefacts that shorten estimate-to-payment without multiplying admin headcount wherever avoidable—sometimes public web, increasingly operator consoles and guarded automations stitched to existing accounting discipline.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Professional services demanding gravity</strong> (legal, advisory, fiduciary-adjacent)</summary>

**Symptoms voiced:** reputation drives discovery; parity of digital gravity lags stature; confidentiality anxiety constrains tooling choices informally—not through policy clarity.

**What research sharpens:** who must authenticate whom; permissible retention arcs; interplay between reassurance (credentials, precedent, responsiveness) and operational tempo.

**Closing the breach:** structured intake respecting boundaries; phased disclosure of competence; searchable depth without careless exposure surfaces; integrations that minimise shadow IT while preserving partner judgment—often less “marketing theatre” than **coherent reassurance architecture**.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Logistics, transport & supply-adjacent operations</strong></summary>

**Symptoms voiced:** qualification happens asynchronously; tenders favour operators who assemble proof swiftly; brittle proof-of-insurance circulation.

**What research sharpens:** trust gates before negotiation; artefacts expected at each gate; duplication between bid desk and dispatched reality.

**Closing the breach:** qualification spine—documentation, SLA narrative, repeatable proof—with minimal redundancy to field updates; selectively automating only where signal quality already supports it.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Automotive speciality & aftermarket craft</strong></summary>

**Symptoms voiced:** brand visible socially; transactional spine underdeveloped; aftermarket nuance drowned by generic quoting templates ill-suited to bespoke work.

**What research sharpens:** decision tree from curiosity consultative sale; photographic evidence flows; recurring relationship versus one-off uplift.

**Closing the breach:** narrative-led surfaces tied to **booking logic that respects ambiguity**, with **staged** capture of vehicle condition—rather than an appointment widget orphaned from operational truth.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Construction & allied skilled trades portfolios</strong></summary>

**Symptoms voiced:** portfolio wins attention; narrowing from curiosity to sanctioned spend remains frictionful; subcontractors multiply coordination edges.

**What research sharpens:** role of specification packets; proof artefacts (insurance bonding, phased draws); escalation paths when scope mutates reality.

**Closing the breach:** structured inquiry capture aligned to phased commercial reality—less brochure excess; more **credential-aware momentum**, respecting how large commitments actually authorise internally.

</details>

**If your industry or trade sits outside these vignettes**, the operative question persists: **is there measurable waste, mistrust latency or cohesion debt we could credibly dissolve with disciplined research-backed engineering?**
